BEYONCÉ



There is no other recording artist I would ever do this assignment on other than one of the world's greatest performers to ever exist. She is too classy for this world and forever that girl. Her expansive twenty-plus recording career includes a plenitude of iconic hits, acclaimed albums, and moments that will forever be symbolic of the icon that she is.

Biography

Beyoncé Giselle Knowles-Carter, otherwise known as "Queen Bey" was born and raised in Houston, Texas on September 4th, 1981. Beyoncé's ancestry is African-American on her father’s side and French Creole on her mother’s. Beyoncé’s families were modest business owners and salespersons before leaving their efforts to help Beyoncé and her younger sister Solange pursue music careers in 1989. Beyoncé would later become a part of Destiny’s Child. In 1997, Destiny’s Child broke into the mainstream with "No, No, No (Pt. 2.)" Destiny's Child would continue to release hit albums and singles before she would start to release music on her own in 2003.

Timeline

  • 1995 - Destiny’s Child gets signed and later gets dropped from Elektra Records.
  • 1997 - Destiny’s Child signs with Columbia Records. Destiny Child breakthrough the industry.
  • 2000 - Beyoncé wins her first Grammy for "Best R&B Song" for "Say My Name."
  • 2002 - Beyoncé is the first black woman to win ASCAP Songwriter of the Year.
  • 2002 - Beyoncé begins a relationship with rapper and mogul, Jay-Z (Shawn Carter.)
  • 2003 - Beyoncé drops first solo effort "Dangerously In Love."
  • 2005 - Destiny’s Child breaks up.
  • 2008 - Beyoncé marries Jay-Z.
  • 2008 - Beyoncé’s "Single Ladies (Put a Ring on It)" becomes one of the first viral music videos on YouTube.
  • 2010 - Beyoncé wins "Song of the Year" for "Single Ladies" at the Grammy Awards. Beyoncé also breaks the Grammy record for most wins in one night for a female artist (6.)
  • 2012 - Beyoncé gives birth to her first child (Blue Ivy Carter.)
  • 2013 - Beyoncé is the first artist to create a visual album. "BEYONCÉ" was dropped with no industry promotion. The record changed the industry’s landscape for releasing commercial music.
  • 2016 - Beyoncé reaches new career heights with "Lemonade." "Lemonade" becomes Beyonce’s first record to gain critical acclaim status among reviewers.
  • 2017 - Beyoncé wins her first Peabody award for "Lemonade."
  • 2017 - Beyoncé’s twins are born (Rumi and Sir.)
  • 2018 - Beyoncé and her husband create a group and release collaborative music.
  • 2021 - Beyoncé becomes the most-awarded female in Grammy history.
  • 2022 - Beyoncé yet again redefines the music industry with her latest record, "Renaissance."
  • 2022 - Beyoncé becomes the most nominated female in Grammy history.

Summary

Beyonce is a recording artist, singer, songwriter, fashion designer, philanthropist, icon, and performer who’s twelve American number-one singles (eight solos, four with Destiny’s Child) and eight American number-one albums (seven solos, one with destiny child.) have netted her countless acclaim and accolades in the music industry and the public. Her talent, longevity, and innovations in music do not compare to any other artist in the field. To this day, Beyonce remains peerless, as many others look to her (like me) for inspiration in their craft and lives.
